Creating an Assisted Tummy Time Experience

For this project, our goal was to create a device to assist an infant with hypotonia in doing their tummy time exercises. Our solution was the Tummy Time Trianles, a set of foam ramps that supported the infant during tummy time, provided engaging stimuli to encourage movement, and were small, easy-to-set-up, and repurposable.

The Importance of Tummy Time

Tummy time is important for infants to develop muscle tone. However, some infants have hypotonia, extremely low muscle tone that prevents them from engaging in these exercises at all. When this is the case, doctors recommend doing supported tummy time, where infants can do these exercises with an aid to better support themselves. Initially, our clients (the infant's parents), used a propped-up mattress to support their child during tummy time, which was bulky and inconveient to set up and use.

Initial Lo-Fi Mockups

Ramp Mockup

Our ramp mockup aimed to support the user in a similar way to a propped up mattress, by providing an incline to support them through tummy time. This design is much smaller and easier to use than a large, bulky mattress and still provides the necessary support for the infant during tummy time.

Pillow Mockup

Our pillow mockup aimed to support the infant by providing a cushion for their arms while doing their tummy time exercises. It consisted of a larger cushion to support their arms as well as a smaller cushion on the front to catch their head if they fell during tummy time. However, we enetually eliminated this design as it was less intuitive to use and was not as supportive as the ramp mockup.

Making Tummy Time Fun

To keep the infant engaged and encourage movement during tummy time, we developed a removeable cover that provided both auditory and tactile stimuli for our user. Their parents indicated that they were very interested in sounds and soft materials. To encourage movement, we inserted a piece of cellophane into the cover that would crinkle when the user moved their body. Similarly, to encourage the infant to move their arms, we included three felt squares on the outside of the cover that they could reach out and feel during tummy time.

Additionally, we included three loops on the front edge of the cover for easy attachment of their favorite clip-on toys and rattles, things their parents say they use to keep their child engaged during tummy time.

Adjustable Assistance

The Tummy Time Triangles consists of two foam ramps that can either be stacked on each other or used independently, depndending on how much support the infant needs. As they grow stronger, one of the ramps can be removed to provide less support without doing tummy time on a completely flat surface.

Adjustable Assistance

The Tummy Time Triangles consists of two foam ramps that can either be stacked on each other or used independently, depndending on how much support the infant needs. As they grow stronger, one of the ramps can be removed to provide less support without doing tummy time on a completely flat surface.

Additionally, these ramps can be repurposed into reading pillows, something our clients indicated they would be interested in as they have two growing children and this product is only necessary for a short period of time.

On campus, I was heavily involved in the Responsible AI Student Organization (RAISO) as the newsletter committee executive - read more about our work here. In addition, I also worked as a TA for Intro to Human-Computer Interaction and as a peer coach through the Undergraduate Program for Advancing Learning (UPAL).

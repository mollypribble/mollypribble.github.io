SaveMiHeart Conference Logo

Date - March 2021

Client - SaveMiHeart

Role - Graphic Designer

Skills - Adobe Illustrator

About

SaveMiHeart is a nonprofit organization based in Michigan that aims to improve survival from sudden cardiac arrest in the community through research and education initiatives. For their 6th annual conference, they needed a logo that would both matched their branding as well as illustrate what the process of surviving sudden cardiac arrest looks like.

The Process

Below is the process of developing the SaveMiHeart conference logo, from ideation to final product. Hover over the images to learn more about tthe development of this project.

Chain of Survival

My client indicated they would like to include the chain of surviving sudden cardiac arrest in the logo.

Colors

The colors for this project were pulled from SaveMiHeart's existing logo and branding.

EKG Line

The EKG line showing healthy heartbeats also inspired the design of the conference logo.

SaveMiHeart Logo

The SaveMiHeart logo and branding inspiring the look of the conference logo.

Initial Sketches

The initial sketches depicted the chain of survival as part of an EKG line.

Adding CPR

After client feedback, CPR hands were added into the chain of survival in the logo.

Final Logo

The final logo depicts the chain of cardiac arrest survival on an EKG line. The colors are drawn from the colors from the existing SaveMiHeart logo. The final logo also includes the SaveMiHeart logo and slogan as well as the name of the conference, which includes stylistic details that match the logo.

Raising Awareness of Sudden Cardiac Arrest

SaveMiHeart is a nonprofit organization based in Michigan that aims to improve survival from sudden cardiac arrest in the community through research and education initiatives. This year they are virtually hosting their 6th annual conference covering bystander intervention and cardiac arrest survival after the COVID-19 pandemic.

For their conference, SaveMiheart needed a logo that would both match their branding as well as illustrate what the process of surviving sudden cardiac arrest looks like.

Adding CPR

After client feedback, CPR hands were added into the chain of survival in the logo.

The Final Product

Below is the final version of the logo. It consisted of SaveMiHeart's logo and the conference title arranged around a stylized EKG line depicting the chain of surviving sudden cardiac arrest. In addition to matching existing SaveMiHeart branding, this logo also successfully depicts the story of the surviving sudden cardiac arrest and also provides an informative visualization of the chain of survival.

Final Logo

The final logo depicts the chain of cardiac arrest survival on an EKG line. The colors are drawn from the colors from the existing SaveMiHeart logo. The final logo also includes the SaveMiHeart logo and slogan as well as the name of the conference, which includes stylistic details that match the logo.

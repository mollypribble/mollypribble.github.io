Date - March 2021

About

SaveMiHeart is a nonprofit organization based in Michigan that aims to improve survival from sudden cardiac arrest in the community through research and education initiatives. For their 6th annual conference, they needed a logo that would both matched their branding as well as illustrate what the process of surviving sudden cardiac arrest looks like.
